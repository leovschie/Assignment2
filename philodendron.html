PHILODENDRON

Philodendrons

Philodendron is a large genus of flowering plants in the Araceae family. The genus of philodendrons comprises some of the most popular houseplants today. Examples are the well-known Monstera Deliciosa, with the holes in the leaves, or the hanging plant Philodendron Scandens.


Philodendron Guide

  • Light: No direct sunlight, light or half-shade
  • Water: Around once a week, rewater once soil starts to feel dry
  • Spraying the leaves: Once a week
  • Repotting: Once a year
  • Fun fact: Has air-purifying qualities
