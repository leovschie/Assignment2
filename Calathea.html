CALATHEA

Calatheas

Calathea is a genus of plants that belong to the family Marantaceae. There are several dozen species in this genus. They are also some of the most popular houseplants due to their decorative leaves and many different colours.

Calathea Guide

  • Light: No direct sunlight, prefers a place in the shadow
  • Water: Once or twice a week, soil should remain slightly moist
  • Spraying the leaves: Twice a week
  • Repotting: Once every one or two years
  • Fun fact: Calatheas close their leaves during the night
